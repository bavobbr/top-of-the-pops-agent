About

What is this?

Top of the Pops is an AI-powered visual learning app that helps you study ranked lists across any category. Enter a topic, and AI generates a ranked list with images, descriptions, and key facts for each item.

How it works

Uses Google's Gemini AI to generate educational content and Wikipedia for relevant images. Supports 20 languages for content generation.
